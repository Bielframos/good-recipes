# Carrot Cake

Carrot cake is a classic, soft-textured cake with a lightly sweet taste, made with grated carrots and topped with a generous layer of chocolate frosting.

<PreparationTime total="40 minutes" preparation="15 minutes" cooking="25 minutes" />

## Ingredientes

- 3 medium carrots
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1 pinch of salt
- 150g semi-sweet chocolate
- 1 can of heavy cream

---

## Preparation

1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F and grease a baking pan with butter and flour.
2. Peel and chop the carrots into small pieces.
3. In a blender, blend the carrots, eggs, and vegetable oil until smooth.
4. In a large bowl, mix sugar, flour, baking powder, and salt.
5. Pour the blender mixture into the bowl with dry ingredients and stir until smooth.
6. Pour the batter into the baking pan and bake for about 40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
7. While the cake bakes, prepare the chocolate frosting: Melt the chocolate in a double boiler or in the microwave, then mix with heavy cream until smooth.
8. Once baked, let the cake cool before removing from the pan and spread the chocolate frosting on top.
